Private Shows

Want your group to always remember the next outing you throw for them?

Then book The Hideout Theatre for your next event. Our professional entertainers will craft a laugh-filled, interactive theatrical experience of any length for any size group that will leave your folks wanting more. It’s guaranteed to be unlike any “show” you’ve ever seen.

The Classic

Our classic improv comedy show is perfect for your company event, holiday party or conference. Four of the best improv comedians in Austin (plus a host and a musician) will perform a 45 to 75-minute performance of interactive games, scenes, and a high-energy improvised movie in the style of the audience’s choosing. We can host you at our theater on Congress Ave downtown (seats up to 95) or bring our show to a space that better accommodates your group.
