Towers of Hanoi

Drag and Drop Game Using Javascript/JQuery

This game starts with ascending sized rings and 3 columns. You can drag and drop the rings to the different columns. The goal is to take the stack of rings and move it to the bottom stack. You can only place a ring in an empty column or on top of a larger ring. You cannot place a ring on top of a smaller ring. The game is coded to prevent bad moves and knows when the game is won. (All the rings stacked at the bottom largest on the left to smallest on the right.)
