Looking for a fun night out
for your Group?

Look No Further

We’ve been giving people a good time in downtown Austin every weekend since 1999. We provide top-notch improv comedy in our centrally located theatre complex, which features two performance spaces and a lounge complete with beer, wine, desserts, coffee, pizza, and more. So whether you’ve got a bachelorette party, bachelor party, birthday party, company outing, school trip, or whatever, we’ve got you covered. We happily offer group discounts to parties of 10 or more. We also provide you with a variety of customizable options to make your night even more special.

We Will Make Your Group Party Amazing!

  • Want drinks for everyone in your group? $5 per person gets either beer or champagne for every member of your party!
  • Want to pre-order cupcakes for everyone? We can make that happen.
  • Want party favors? We’ve got those too.
  • Want your person of honor to be the center of attention in an improv game? We can do that.
